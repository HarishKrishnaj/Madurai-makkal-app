Problem Statement

The Problems We Address

Real civic and environmental challenges in Madurai that our AI-driven solution tackles head-on.

Unorganized Waste Collection

Lack of real-time tracking and route optimization for waste collection vehicles leads to missed pickups and overflowing bins across neighborhoods.

Inefficient Complaint Systems

Existing grievance portals are cumbersome, slow, and lack AI-powered categorization â€” making it hard for citizens to report cleanliness issues effectively.

Poor Waste Segregation

Most households and establishments lack awareness and tools for proper segregation of wet, dry, and hazardous waste.

No Data-Driven Decision Making

Municipal authorities lack real-time dashboards and predictive analytics to allocate resources efficiently and identify cleanliness hotspots.
